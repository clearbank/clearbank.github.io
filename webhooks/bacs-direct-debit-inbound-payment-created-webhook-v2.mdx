**This sends a webhook notification confirming that a Bacs Direct Debit inbound payment has been created. **

On receipt of a direct debit request from a customer bank or its
business, this webhook notifies that the debit will be paid out of its
account on the following day.

The bank can use this to inform the debit account holder to expect the
debit, and ensure that there are sufficient funds to cover it. If they
want to oppose the debit, they should contact ClearBank.



#### Request body

```json
{
    "Type": "BacsDirectDebitInboundPaymentCreatedV2",
    "Version": 2,
    "Payload": {...},
    "Nonce":
}
```
