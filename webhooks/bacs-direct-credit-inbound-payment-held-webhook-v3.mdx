**This sends a webhook notification confirming that a Bacs Direct Credit inbound payment has been held.**

Before a credit is settled, it undergoes regulatory screening against
sanctions lists, or for key risk indicators. If there is anything
suspicious, it is held for review, and this webhook is returned. It is
normally followed by the ***transaction-settled*** webhook when the
payment is released.


#### Request body

```json
{
    "Type": "BacsDirectCreditInboundPaymentHeldV3",
    "Version": 3,
    "Payload": {...},
    "Nonce":
}
```
