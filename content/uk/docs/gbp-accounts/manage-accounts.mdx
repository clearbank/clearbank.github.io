## Real GBP accounts

A real account is a physical account held with ClearBank and can be used to send and/or receive payments.

**Before creating an account, please check which [account type](./account-types#account-types) you need.** If you are unsure, please get in touch.

The balance of a real account is also held by ClearBank and can be viewed via the Institution portal and the ClearBank API. Real accounts are reconciled for you by ClearBank.

## Virtual GBP accounts

**Virtual accounts are held on your platform, but their funds are kept in a real account held with ClearBank.**  
To create a virtual account with ClearBank, you must create it under one of your real general (also known as pooled) accounts. You can then use this account's virtual IBAN (vIBAN) to send and receive payments using funds from the associated real account.  
You can create virtual accounts using the **[POST /v2/Accounts/\{accountId\}/Virtual](#create-accounts-virtual)** endpoint.  

You are responsible for managing and reconciling your virtual accounts' balances.

## Payment accounts

Payment accounts facilitate limited payment transactions for retail customers. By default, they support:

* Receipt of payments from other account holders and third parties
* Regular outbound via standing orders and direct debits
* Open Banking (and therefore must be provided through a platform managed by a regulated firm)
* Joint account ownership

In a limited capacity, payment accounts _may_ support:

* Debit cards enabled for Point of Sale (PoS) transactions
* Anytime bank transfers
* Low interest accrual

ClearBank may grant these features to your payment accounts by exception, potentially at the expense of one or more default features.

You can create a Payment account for a customer by using our [**POST accounts/sterling/v1/payment-accounts**](#create-a-payment-or-current-account) endpoint.

## Current accounts

Current accounts provide extensive payment functionality for customers. Out of the box, they support:

* Debit cards enabled for cash withdrawals and Point of Sale (PoS) transactions
* May send or receive payments with other account holders and third parties
* Anytime bank transfers
* Low interest accrual
* Open Banking (and therefore must be provided through a platform managed by a regulated firm)
* Joint account ownership
* Account switching via Pay.UK's Current Account Switch Service (CASS)

You can create a Payment account for a customer by using our [**POST accounts/sterling/v1/payment-accounts**](#create-a-payment-or-current-account) endpoint.
