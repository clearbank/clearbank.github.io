## Retail customer KYC

<Callout colour="green">
<h2>KYC is coming soon</h2>
<p>All information on this page describes a solution that is <strong>not yet live in production and therefore could change</strong>. This includes any endpoint and webhook details. The information is being provided early to aid you in integrating with our API in our UK simulation environment.</p>
</Callout>

Using our retail customer KYC endpoint makes it easy to meet your due diligence requirements when onboarding customers. By simply sharing key customer information, you can conduct know your customer checks and receive the results via webhook.

For convenience, we'll also use the provided information to register a new customer if the checks succeed.

If you'd like access to this product, please speak to your Client Director.

<Callout colour="blue">
  This service can only conduct KYC checks on customers who:<br /><br />
  • Currently live in the UK<br />
  • Have tax residency in the UK<br />
  • Do not have dual tax residency with another country<br />
  • Are not US nationals
</Callout>

## Simulating KYC checks

You can simulate successful and declined KYC checks in our simulation environment.  
To do this, provide one of the below keywords to the [**POST /v1/customercheck/retail**](#kyc-check-and-create-a-new-retail-customer) endpoint:
* To simulate a successful KYC check, set the customer's `surname` field to `pass`
* To simulate a declined KYC check, set the customer's `surname` field to `fail`\*

\* - Note: You'll need to coordinate with our API Integration team to simulate a declined KYC check, as steps are required our side to send the Retail Customer Application Declined webhook.
