## Payment accounts overview

Payment accounts are regular GBP accounts with more payment functionality than savings accounts. With a payment account, your customers may set up direct debits, standing orders, and make day-to-day payments - among other features.

Payment accounts come in two subtypes: **Current accounts** and **Transaction accounts**:

* [Current accounts](#current-accounts) are fully featured but must comply with Payment Account Regulations (PARs) and Payment Service Regulations (PSRs).
* [Transaction accounts](#transaction-accounts) have fewer features but are subject to lighter regulations (PSRs, but not PARs).

Payment accounts are available to embedded banking partners by prior agreement with ClearBank. If you're interested in using this account type, please contact your Client Director.

<Callout colour="blue">
  <strong>Note:</strong> Deposits held in Payment accounts may be eligible for FSCS protection. FSCS protection insures deposits of up to £120,000 per person per authorised firm (ClearBank) in the event of insolvency. Customers should review the specific terms and conditions of their account to confirm whether FSCS protection applies.
</Callout>

## Current accounts

Current accounts provide extensive payment functionality for customers. Out of the box, they support:

* Receipt of payments from other account holders and third parties
* Regular outbound payments via standing orders and direct debits
* Anytime bank transfers
* Open Banking
* Joint account ownership
* Account switching

You can create a current account for a customer by calling our [**POST /accounts/sterling/v1/payment-accounts**](#create-a-transaction-or-current-account) endpoint, and specifying `"CurrentAccount"` as the `accountSubType`.

## Transaction accounts

Transaction accounts facilitate limited payment transactions for retail customers, but they are subject to fewer regulations (PSRs and not PARs). By default, transaction accounts support:

* Receipt of payments from other account holders and third parties
* Regular outbound payments via standing orders and direct debits
* Open Banking
* Joint account ownership

In limited configurations, transaction accounts _may_ support:

* Anytime bank transfers

On request, ClearBank may grant additional payment features for transaction accounts. Contact your Client Director for more details.

You can create a transaction account for a customer by calling our [**POST /accounts/sterling/v1/payment-accounts**](#create-a-payment-account) endpoint, and specifying `"TransactionAccount"` as the `accountSubType`.
