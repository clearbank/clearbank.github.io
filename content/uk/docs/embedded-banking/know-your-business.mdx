## Retail customer KYB

Using our business customer KYB endpoint makes it easy to meet your due diligence requirements when onboarding customers. By simply sharing key customer information, you can conduct know your business checks and receive the results via webhook.

For convenience, we'll also use the provided information to register a new business customer if the checks succeed.

If you'd like access to this product, please speak to your Client Director.

<Callout colour="blue">
  This service can only conduct KYB checks on businesses who:<br /><br />
  • Currently live in the UK<br />
  • Have tax residency in the UK<br />
  • Do not have dual tax residency with another country<br />
  • Are not US nationals
</Callout>
