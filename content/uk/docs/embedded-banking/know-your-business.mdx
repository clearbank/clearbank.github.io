## Retail customer KYB

Using our business customer KYB endpoint makes it easy to meet your due diligence requirements when onboarding customers. By simply sharing key customer information, you can conduct know your business checks on limited companies and receive the results via webhook.

For convenience, we'll also use the provided information to register the new business customer if the checks succeed.

If you'd like access to this product, please contact your Client Director.
