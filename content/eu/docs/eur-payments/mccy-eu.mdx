## Multi-currency EU

Our Multi-currency Payments EU (MCCY EU) service lets you make multi-currency cross-border payments directly from a EUR account. When you send an MCCY EU payment, your EUR is converted into the target currency automatically. This simplifies multi-currency payments by allowing you to make payments in a wide range of currencies using a straightforward interface.

To get started with MCCY EU, you'll need a ClearBank EU euro account. This will be set up for you as part of your onboarding process.

If you're interested in using this service, please reach out to your Client Director.

## Availability and hours

### Payment properties

* All MCCY EU payments settle same day.
* MCCY EU payments have a minimum payment amount of 50 EUR.
* This is a cross-border service: you can send MCCY payments worldwide.

### Available currencies

MCCY EU support several target currencies, these include:

* Canadian Dollar (CAD)
* Swiss Franc (CHF)
* Czech Koruna (CZK)
* Danish Krone (DKK)
* Pound Sterling (GBP)
* Hungarian Forint (HUF)
* Norwegian Krone (NOK)
* Polish Złoty (PLN)
* Romanian Leu (RON)
* Swedish Krona (SEK)
* United States Dollar (USD)

### Hours and holidays

Please note that each currency's availability is subject to different national holidays and respective cutoff times. In general, you can make MCCY payments Monday to Friday, 9:30am to 3:30pm Central European Time (CET).

## Payment process

### Request a payment

Payment requests should be submitted to our **POST /payments/cross-border/v1/payments** endpoint. We'll respond to a valid request synchronously with a **200 - OK** response, with a payload confirming details of the requested payment. The payment is only proposed at this stage; we will not proceed with sending the payment until you accept it.

### Accept a payment

To proceed with a requested payment, you must accept it using our **POST /payments/cross-border/v1/payments/accept** endpoint using the UETR of your original request. If we do not receive acceptance within 60 seconds of the original **200 - OK** response's `paymentValueDate`, the payment request will automatically expire. For clarity, an explicit `expiryDateTime` property is also included in the **200 - OK** response.

### Abandon a payment

If you do not want to proceed with a requested payment, no action is required. Requested payments will expire after **60 seconds** (when the `expiryDateTime` has passed). You won't be notified when a payment request expires; if you try to accept a requested payment after the `expiryDateTime` has passed, you'll receive a **400 - Bad Request** error.

### Ensure settlement amount

You can 'fix' either side of the currency pair in an MCCY EU payment.

* To fix the amount of EUR you'll pay, use the instructedAmount object to declare a payment amount in EUR, including fees. The amount the creditor will receive will be confirmed in the **200 - OK** response body, under the property interbankSettlementAmount.
* To fix the amount of target currency - for example, GBP - use the interbankSettlementAmount object to declare the settlement amount in GBP after transaction fees. The amount of EUR you'll pay will be confirmed in the **200 - OK** response body, under the property instructedAmount.

### Receive payment updates

We'll update you about payments' statuses via webhook. MCCY EU has three such webhooks:

* [CBEU Cross-border Outbound Payment Accepted webhook](#cbeu-cross-border-outbound-payment-accepted-webhook)
* [CBEU Cross-border Outbound Payment Completed webhook](#cbeu-cross-border-outbound-payment-completed-webhook)
* [CBEU Cross-border Outbound Payment Failed webhook](#cbeu-cross-border-outbound-payment-failed-webhook)
