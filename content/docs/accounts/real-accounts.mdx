## Real Accounts

A real account is a physical account held with ClearBank速 and can be used to send and/or receive payments.
The balance of a real account is also held by ClearBank速 and can be viewed via the Institution portal and the ClearBank速 API. Real accounts are reconciled for you by ClearBank速.
