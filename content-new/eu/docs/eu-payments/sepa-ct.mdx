## SEPA Credit Transfer

SEPA CT (Single Euro Payments Area Credit Transfer) is a payment scheme that enables you to move Euros between scheme participants. Transactions are capped at €999,999,999 per payment.

You can send, receive, recall, and return SEPA CT payments. 

### Opening hours

Inbound SEPA CT payments can be received on Netherlands business days between 02:30 and 16:30 CET.

Outbound SEPA CT payments can be sent on Netherlands business days between 07:00 and 16:00 CET. Transactions submitted after 16:00 or on bank holidays or weekends will be rejected. If a payment is submitted before the cut-off point but cannot be submitted to scheme, it will be placed in a queue until outbound payments reopen.

## Sending a SEPA Credit Transfer payment

To send a payment, use the [/payments/sepa-ct/v1/customer-payments](#send-a-sepa-ct-payment) endpoint. You will receive the SEPA CT Outbound Payment Completed webhook and the [Customer Accounts Transaction Settled webhook](#customer-accounts-transaction-completed-webhook).

## SEPA Credit Transfer recalls

### Sending recalls

You can send SEPA recall requests using the [Request a SEPA CT payment recall](#request-a-sepa-ct-payment-recall) endpoint. Recalls can take up to 15 business days. **How will they know if a recall has been accepted/rejected?**

<EndpointBlock
  type="post"
  title="Request a SEPA CT payment recall"
  endpoints={[
    {
      path: "/payments/sepa-ct/v1/recall",
      version: "1.0-SEPA-CT"
    }
  ]}
/>

### Receiving recalls

You may receive a recall request via the [Recall Request webhook](#respond-to-sepa-ct-recall-request). To respond to the recall request, use the [/v1/payments/sepa/{paymentId}/recall-response](#respond-to-a-sepa-CT-payment-recall-request) endpoint.

Recall requests should be responded to within 15 business days.

<Callout colour="blue">
If you accept the recall, a pacs.004 payment will be automatically initiated.  You do not need to initiate a payment.
</Callout>

<EndpointBlock
  type="post"
  title="Respond to SEPA CT recall request"
  endpoints={[
    {
      path: "/payments/sepa-ct/v1/recall-response",
      version: "1.0-SEPA-CT"
    }
  ]}
/>

### Recall reason codes

Recalls can be requested using the following reason codes:

| Reason | Description|Time limit to send recall request|
|------|-----------|---------------------------------|
|DUPL |Duplicate sending|10 buiness days| 
|TECH |Technical problems resulting in an erroneous SEPA Instant Transaction|10 buiness days|
|FRAD |Fraudulent originated SEPA Instant Instruction|13 months|
|AC03 |Wrong account number in original transfer|13 months|
|AM09 |Wrong amount in original transfer|13 months|
|CUST |Requested by customer|13 months|

## SEPA Credit Transfer returns

SEPA CT payments can also be returned to the payment originator.

You can return a payment using the [/payments/sepa-ct/v1/payment-returns](#return-a-sepa-ct-payment) endpoint. You can only return a payment after receving the [SEPA CT Inbound Payment Completed webhook] #sepa-ct-inbound-payment-completed-webhook. Returns must be sent within three business days of the original payment's settlement date.
